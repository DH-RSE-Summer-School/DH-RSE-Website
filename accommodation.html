Accommodation in Manchester

Recommended accommodation for visitors attending the DH & RSE Summer School 2026 at the University of Manchester. All hotels are listed with approximate walking times to the Booth Street East, off Oxford Road — the venue for the Summer School.

Prices listed are indicative typical rates and should be used for planning purposes only. Always check current availability and prices on booking platforms. Ratings are sourced from Google Maps.
